Niflheim

Cold earth

Only cowardly, dead men who die in their sleep (not in battle) end up here. This is the home of Niddhog, a dragon that feeds on tree roots.

The depths of Niflhjálm are mentioned in Baldr's dreams. Odin himself visits Niflhjálm to inquire about the bad dreams of his son, Baldr, and there meets Garm, the dog of Hel, which possibly suggests a connection between this world and Helheim.
