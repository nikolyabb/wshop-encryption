There are two teams: the Encrypting Team and the Decrypting Team.
The Encrypting Team needs to encrypt a word and prevent the Decrypting Team from deciphering it.
The Decrypting Team has 15 minutes to decrypt the word. During this time, they may ask for one hint from the Encrypting Team.
If the word remains undecrypted, the Encrypting Team wins.
If the word is successfully decrypted, the Decrypting Team wins .
