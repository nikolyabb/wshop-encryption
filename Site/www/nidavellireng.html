Nidavellir

Home of the dwarves

Some believe that this kingdom was actually founded by a blind man named Hjörðr, who was banished from Ásgard because it was prophesied that he would kill Baldur.

But travelers see no sign of this. Since the dwarves are clever magicians, this realm is a source of wondrous things.
