# 📂 TEAMS BRIEFING KIT

> **Instructions for the Teacher:** > 1. Choose your Story Mode (A: Zombies, B: Magic, or C: AI/Matrix).
> 2. Print the **Universal Rules** for everyone.
> 3. Print the specific **Role Cards** for the chosen story and hand them to the teams.

---

## 📜 Universal Rules (For All Teams)
1.  **The Delegate:** Pick **ONE** person to be your Delegate. Only they can stand up and hand papers to the next team.
2.  **Safety Perimeter:** Stay **1 meter** away from other teams. No blocking or tackling.
3.  **Communication:** * **Team 1 & 3:** You cannot speak to each other.
    * **Team 2:** You can listen, look, and spy.
4.  **Winning:** To win, you must shout the Victory Phrase and the correct Secret Word.

## 🧟 OPTION A: PROTOCOL ZERO (Sci-Fi / Zombies)

### Team 1: The Scientists (Sender)
* **Location:** The Lab (Trapped).
* **Goal:** Encrypt the Cure Formula so the Military can make it, but the Infected cannot corrupt it.
* **Action:**
    1.  Pick a word from the **Formula Bank**.
    2.  Encrypt it using the **Cipher of the Round**.
    3.  Hand the paper to the Infected (Team 2).
    4.  **Do not** explain the message!

### Team 2: The Infected (Interceptor)
* **Location:** The Hallway (Blocking the path).
* **Goal:** Intercept the formula and corrupt it.
* **Action:**
    1.  Receive the paper from the Scientists.
    2.  You have **180 Seconds** (or until the GM stops you).
    3.  **Strategy:** You may make a **COPY** of the code on your own paper.
    4.  When time is up, you **MUST** hand the *original* paper to the Military.
    5.  Keep trying to crack your copy! If you solve it first, shout: **“INTERCEPTED! [WORD]”**

### Team 3: The Military (Receiver)
* **Location:** Extraction Point.
* **Goal:** Receive the formula and synthesize the cure.
* **Action:**
    1.  Wait for the paper to arrive.
    2.  **Pressure:** Yell at the Infected to hurry up! ("The virus is spreading! Give us the data!")
    3.  When you get the paper, decrypt it using your **Key**.
    4.  If you solve it first, shout: **“SECURED! [WORD]”**
