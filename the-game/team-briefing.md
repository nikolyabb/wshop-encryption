# Team Briefing

> **Print these instructions and hand them to the respective groups.**

---

## 🕵️ Group 1 – The Field Spies

- **Goal:** Send a secret message to Headquarters (Group 3) without being detected by the Interceptors (Group 2).
- **Instructions:**
  1. Pick a word from the **Word Bank**.
  2. Encrypt it using the **secret code (Cipher)** for this round.
  3. Write the encrypted message clearly on a piece of paper.
  4. Hand the paper to the Interceptors (Group 2).
  5. **Do not** explain what the message says.

---

## 🕵️ Group 2 – The Interceptors

- **Goal:** Crack the code and reveal the secret message before time runs out.
- **Instructions:**
  1. Receive the paper from the Spies.
  2. You have **60 seconds** (or until the timer stops).
  3. Look at the Word Bank and the message. Guess what it says.
  4. If you solve it, shout **“INTERCEPTED!”** and state the word.
  5. If time runs out, hand the paper to Headquarters (Group 3).

---

## 🕵️ Group 3 – Headquarters (HQ)

- **Goal:** Decrypt the message to verify the information is secure.
- **Instructions:**
  1. Wait for the paper to arrive from the Interceptors.
  2. Use your **Secret Key** to convert the symbols back into letters.
  3. Read the word out loud to confirm the message was received successfully.

---

**Good luck, and keep the lines of communication tight!**
